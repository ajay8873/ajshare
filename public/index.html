How to Share Files (Read Carefully)

  • For High-Speed App-to-App Transfer Turn on mobile hotspot on one device and connect the other device to it through Wi-Fi. (This enables direct local file sharing up to 10-15+ Mbps without using internet data).
  • For App-to-Web Transfer If devices are not connected via Hotspot/same Wi-Fi, the app will fallback to WebSocket Relay mode to transfer files over the internet.
Warning: WebSocket Relay mode transfers files over the internet and WILL consume your mobile/internet data plan. Connecting through a local hotspot is highly recommended!

Permissions & Privacy Information

AJShare respects your privacy. We never track, inspect, or store any of your data or files on any server. Files are streamed completely peer-to-peer.

Camera Permission Required to scan the room QR code directly using the built-in scanner to join rooms instantly.
Microphone Permission (Optional) Used on browsers to temporarily bypass mDNS anonymization. This lets us discover raw local IPs so you get high-speed local transfer rates without TURN relay.
Zero Server Storage Your files never touch the cloud or database. The signaling server is only used to connect your devices.
